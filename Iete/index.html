About Us

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) founded in 1953 is one of the leading Professional societies in India.IETE-SF was initiated in 2018 at SVCE with the assistance and involvement of faculty members of the Institution. Its motive is to exhibit and strengthen their technical skills thereby providing a platform for sharing different ideas by conducting various technical and nontechnical events such as webinars, hackathons, and student innovation contests. Conducting prototype series that would provide global exposure and esoteric knowledge through practical experience.
